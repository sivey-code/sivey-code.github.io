Peter Sivey

Reader in Health Economics, University of York

I work at the Centre for Health Economics, University of York and previously worked at RMIT University, La Trobe University, and University of Melbourne in Melbourne, Australia.

My research interests centre on the economics of healthcare markets. I am working on projects including hospital waiting times, emergency department performance, competition in health care, and doctors' labour markets. My wider interests include multinomial choice models, microeconometrics, and microeconomic theory (especially Industrial Organisation) applied to healthcare systems.

I am the Director of ESHCRU, the NIHR Policy Research Unit in the Economics of Health and Care Systems, and co-lead the MEASURE study. I have a PhD in Economics (2009) from the University of York and I am an Associate Editor of Health Economics.

Email: peter.sivey@york.ac.uk
